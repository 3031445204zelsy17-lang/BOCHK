## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- HKMA Taxonomy 是自愿性框架（非强制），旨在促进绿色融资流动
- 与 Common Ground Taxonomy、中国绿债目录和欧盟 Taxonomy 可互操作

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "The HKMA published today (3 May) the Hong Kong Taxonomy for Sustainable Finance (Hong Kong Taxonomy) to enable informed decision making on green and sustainable finance and facilitate relevant finance flows."
> —— 来源：HKMA Press Release, 3 May 2024（英文原文）

> "Currently, it encompasses 12 economic activities under four sectors namely power generation, transportation, construction, and water and waste management."
