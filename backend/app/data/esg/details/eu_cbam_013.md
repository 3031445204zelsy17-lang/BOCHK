## 合规检查项
- [ ] 进口 CBAM 货物的企业（超 50 吨/年净重门槛）是否已申请成为 authorized CBAM declarant
- [ ] 是否指定了间接海关代表（若进口商不在欧盟设立）
- [ ] 2026 年 3 月 31 日前提交申请的进口商可暂继续进口（pending 审批决定）

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：已申请并获批授权 CBAM 申报人
- 部分满足：正在准备申请
- 不满足：未启动，且超额进口将面临处罚

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 门槛为每年进口净重超 50 吨（de minimis，仅适用于钢铁/铝/水泥/化肥；电力和氢无门槛）
- 非欧盟企业必须指定间接海关代表（Indirect Customs Representative），增加合规成本
- 授权申报人可委托第三方提交 CBAM 申报（2025/2083 新增 Art. 5(7a)），但法律责任仍属申报人

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "1. Any importer established in a Member State shall, prior to importing goods into the customs territory of the Union, apply for the status of authorised CBAM declarant ('application for an authorisation')."
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2025/2083, Art. 1(4)(a)，替换 Reg. 2023/956 Art. 5(1)（英文原文，OJ L 2025/2083, 17.10.2025）

> "1a. An indirect customs representative shall obtain the status of authorised CBAM declarant prior to importing goods into the customs territory of the Union."
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2025/2083, Art. 1(4)(b)，插入 Reg. 2023/956 Art. 5(1a)（英文原文）

> "1b. Where Article 2a applies, the importer shall submit the application for an authorisation in cases where that importer expects to exceed the single mass-based threshold."
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2025/2083, Art. 1(4)(b)，插入 Reg. 2023/956 Art. 5(1b)（英文原文）

> "7a. An authorised CBAM declarant may delegate the submission of CBAM declarations as referred to in Article 6 to a person acting on behalf and in the name of that authorised CBAM declarant. The authorised CBAM declarant shall remain responsible for compliance with the obligations applicable to it under this Regulation."
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2025/2083, Art. 1(4)(f)，插入 Reg. 2023/956 Art. 5(7a)（英文原文）

> "an importer or an indirect customs representative that has submitted an application in accordance with Article 5 by 31 March 2026 may provisionally continue to import goods until the competent authority takes a decision"
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2025/2083, Art. 1(13)(d)，插入 Reg. 2023/956 Art. 17(7a)（英文原文）
