## 合规检查项
- [ ] 气候披露是否遵循 IFRS S2 的四大支柱：治理、战略、风险管理、指标与目标
- [ ] 发行人是否了解 IFRS S2 是 TCFD 建议的接续
- [ ] 发行人是否应用 IFRS S1 中与气候相关的条款

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：全面应用 IFRS S2 要求（按各自时间表）
- 部分满足：已参照 TCFD 但尚未完全过渡至 IFRS S2
- 不满足：无气候披露

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- SGX 不要求发行人做出 IFRS S1/S2 全面合规声明，但鼓励
- Practice Note 7.6 第 4.14 段允许仅遵循气候相关要求的声明

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "The IFRS Sustainability Disclosure Standards build on the recommendations of the Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures ('TCFD'). It aims to be a comprehensive global framework of sustainability-related financial disclosures to meet the needs of capital markets..."
> —— 来源：Practice Note 7.6, para 4.9（英文原文）

> "An issuer that complies with all the requirements in IFRS S1 and IFRS S2 can, but is not mandated to, make an explicit and unreserved statement of compliance with the IFRS Sustainability Disclosure Standards; an issuer that complies with all the requirements in IFRS S2 and the climate-relevant provisions in IFRS S1 can, but is not mandated to, state that it complies with the climate-related requirements in the IFRS Sustainability Disclosure Standards."
> —— 来源：Practice Note 7.6, para 4.14（英文原文）
