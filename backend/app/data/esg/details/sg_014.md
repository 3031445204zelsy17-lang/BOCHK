## 合规检查项
- [ ] 发行人是否了解 IFRS S2 提供的结构性救济（permanent structural reliefs）
- [ ] 发行人是否了解首年报告可享受的过渡性救济（temporary transition reliefs）

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：全面了解各类救济并合规运用
- 部分满足：了解救济存在但不知具体内容
- 不满足：完全不知救济安排

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 结构性救济（永久性）：情景分析时可考虑自身技能和资源；可用合理且可支持的资讯（不承担过度成本或努力）
- 过渡性救济（首年）：可不披露 Scope 3、可不使用 GHG Protocol、可不提供比较数据

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "As part of the (permanent) structural reliefs, an issuer is allowed to: (a) consider its skills, capabilities and resources when determining its approach for its climate-related scenario analysis... (b) use all reasonable and supportable information that is available to the issuer at the reporting date without undue cost or effort..."
> —— 来源：Practice Note 7.6, para 4.19（英文原文）

> "As part of the (temporary) transition reliefs, an issuer (including newly-listed issuers) need not do the following in the first year of reporting applying the IFRS Sustainability Disclosure Standards: (a) provide its Scope 3 GHG emissions; (b) use the Greenhouse Gas Protocol... (c) provide comparative information in respect of the preceding period."
> —— 来源：Practice Note 7.6, para 4.20（英文原文）
