## 合规检查项
- [ ] 是否了解 Scope 1&2 GHG 排放的外部有限保证自 FY2029 起强制
- [ ] 是否有计划逐步扩大外部保证覆盖范围

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：已规划外部保证时间表
- 部分满足：了解要求但未行动
- 不满足：完全不了解

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 2025-08-25 修订延后了外部保证强制时间：所有上市公司 Scope 1&2 有限保证自 FY2029 起（ACRA 新闻稿 2025-08-25）
- 大型非上市公司 Scope 1&2 有限保证延至 FY2032

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "External assurance should be performed in accordance with recognised assurance standards, for example the International Standard on Assurance Engagements (ISAE) 3000 (or any subsequent sustainability-specific standard including the International Standard on Sustainability Assurance (ISSA) 5000)..."
> —— 来源：Practice Note 7.6, §5.6（英文原文，"01 Jan 2026 onwards" 现行版），source_url: https://rulebook.sgx.com/rulebook/practice-note-76-sustainability-reporting-guide

> "External limited assurance for Scope 1 and 2 GHG emissions is deferred to FY2029 for all listed companies."
> —— 来源：SGX RegCo & ACRA, News Release, 25 Aug 2025（英文原文），source_url: https://www.acra.gov.sg/news-events/news-announcements/887/
