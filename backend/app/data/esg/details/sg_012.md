## 合规检查项
- [ ] STI 成分股发行人是否从 FY2026 起强制披露 Scope 3 GHG 排放
- [ ] 非 STI 成分股发行人（自愿）是否了解 Scope 3 15 大类别
- [ ] 排放测量是否依据 GHG Protocol Corporate Value Chain (Scope 3) Standard

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：STI 成分股已合规披露 Scope 3；非 STI 已在建设能力
- 部分满足：STI 已开始但数据质量待提升
- 不满足：STI 未开始

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- STI 成分股 Scope 3 自 FY2026 起**强制**；其他发行人**自愿**
- Scope 3 可按 15 个类别披露不适用的部分
- 可用次级数据（industry-average data）估算

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "If the issuer is an STI Constituent on 30 June 2025, it must disclose its Scope 3 GHG emissions from FYC 2026, even if it ceases to be an STI Constituent subsequently."
> —— 来源：Practice Note 7.6, para 4.12(c)（英文原文，"01 Jan 2026 onwards" 版本）

> "Emissions must be measured in accordance with the Greenhouse Gas Protocol: A Corporate Accounting and Reporting Standards (2004), subject to the reliefs specified and to the extent that it does not conflict with the IFRS Sustainability Disclosure Standards."
> —— 来源：Practice Note 7.6, para 4.21（英文原文）
