## 合规检查项
- [ ] 是否已识别实质性 ESG 因子并说明选择和优先级排序的理由
- [ ] 是否考虑了价值链范围（含外包环节和下游流程）
- [ ] 是否使用"识别—评级—优先级排序—验证"四步流程

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：已建立结构化的实质性评估流程
- 部分满足：有评估但方法不够系统
- 不满足：无实质性评估

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 腐败（corruption）在不少行业可能被忽略，但投资者关注度高
- 多样性（性别/技能/经验）被视为可持续经营的重要社会指标

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "The sustainability report should identify the material ESG factors, and describe both the reasons for and the process of selection, taking into consideration their relevance or impact to the business, strategy, financial planning, business model and key stakeholders."
> —— 来源：Practice Note 7.6, para 4.1(a)（英文原文）

> "The issuer should review its business in the context of the value chain and determine what ESG factors in relation to its interaction with its physical environment and social community and its governance, are material for the continuity of its business."
> —— 来源：Practice Note 7.6, para 4.2（英文原文）
