## 合规检查项
- [ ] 若发行人选择披露 Scope 3，是否按 GHG Protocol 企业价值链（Scope 3）核算与报告标准（2011）中的分类披露
- [ ] 若未披露 Scope 3，是否按"遵守或解释"要求提供了充分的考虑理由

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：披露了 Scope 3 排放量（按排放类别分类），且使用"合理且可支持的信息"并说明了限制
- 部分满足：披露了部分 Scope 3 类别但范围不完整
- 不满足：未披露且未提供充分理由

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- Scope 3 数据收集难度大，价值链排放估算常依赖行业平均值而非具体供应商数据
- 发行人可利用 para 29(b) Note 1 的"合理且可支持的信息"过渡

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "An issuer shall disclose its absolute gross greenhouse gas emissions generated during the reporting period, expressed as metric tons of CO2 equivalent, classified as: ... (c) Scope 3 greenhouse gas emissions."
> —— 来源：Appendix C2, Part D, para 28(c)（英文原文）

> "An issuer shall: ... (c) for Scope 2 greenhouse gas emissions disclosed in accordance with paragraph 28(b), disclose its location-based Scope 2 greenhouse gas emissions, and provide information about any contractual instruments that is necessary to enable an understanding of the issuer's Scope 2 greenhouse gas emissions; and (d) for Scope 3 greenhouse gas emissions disclosed in accordance with paragraph 28(c), disclose the categories included within the issuer's measure of Scope 3 greenhouse gas emissions, in accordance with the Scope 3 categories described in the Greenhouse Gas Protocol Corporate Value Chain (Scope 3) Accounting and Reporting Standard (2011)."
> —— 来源：Appendix C2, Part D, para 29(c)-(d)（英文原文）
