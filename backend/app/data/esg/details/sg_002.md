## 合规检查项
- [ ] 可持续报告是否覆盖全部六大核心要素：(a) 实质性 ESG 因子、(aa) 气候相关披露、(b) 政策实践与绩效、(c) 目标、(d) 报告框架、(e) 董事会声明
- [ ] 是否按"遵守或解释"原则披露（除 711B(1)(aa) 气候披露为强制、711B(2) 不可排除）
- [ ] 气候相关披露是否按 Practice Note 7.6 要求执行

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：全部要素齐全且合规
- 部分满足：要素覆盖不全但已说明
- 不满足：缺失核心要素

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 711B(1)(aa)气候披露自 2025 年起为**强制**（不可"遵守或解释"排除）
- Practice Note 7.6 是气候披露的具体实施指引

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "The sustainability report must describe the sustainability practices with reference to the following primary components:
    (a) material environmental, social and governance factors;
    (aa) climate-related disclosures;
    (b) policies, practices and performance;
    (c) targets;
    (d) sustainability reporting framework; and
    (e) Board statement and associated governance structure for sustainability practices."
> —— 来源：SGX Mainboard Rules, Rule 711B(1)（英文原文，Added 20 Jul 2016, amended 1 Jan 2022, 1 Jan 2025, 1 Jan 2026）

> "The primary component in Rule 711B(1)(aa) must comply with the requirements on climate-related disclosures set out in Practice Note 7.6."
> —— 来源：SGX Mainboard Rules, Rule 711B(3)（英文原文）
