## 合规检查项
- [ ] 发行人是否披露气候相关风险与机会对其报告期财务状况、财务业绩和现金流的影响（定性及定量）
- [ ] 是否说明存在重大调整风险的资产/负债项目
- [ ] 是否披露短期/中期/长期预期财务影响（含投资与处置计划、资金来源）

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：提供定性及定量信息说明当前与预期财务影响，并在无法定量时提供解释
- 部分满足：仅提供定性信息但未解释为何不提供定量信息
- 不满足：未做披露

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 定量财务影响（如减值测试、资产估值受气候因素影响）技术上难度高
- 许多发行人使用 para 25 Notes 3-4 的豁免（不可单独识别或测量不确定性过高），但未充分解释原因

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "An issuer shall disclose qualitative and quantitative information about: (a) how climate-related risks and opportunities have affected its financial position, financial performance and cash flows for the reporting period; and (b) the climate-related risks and opportunities identified in paragraph 24(a) for which there is a significant risk of a material adjustment within the next annual reporting period to the carrying amounts of assets and liabilities reported in the related financial statements."
> —— 来源：Appendix C2, Part D, para 24（英文原文）

> "An issuer need not provide quantitative information about the current or anticipated financial effects of a climate-related risk or opportunity if the issuer determines that: (a) those effects are not separately identifiable; or (b) the level of measurement uncertainty involved in estimating those effects is so high that the resulting quantitative information would not be useful. If an issuer determines that it need not provide quantitative information... the issuer shall: (a) explain why it has not provided quantitative information; (b) provide qualitative information about those financial effects..."
> —— 来源：Appendix C2, Part D, para 25 Notes 3-5（英文原文）
