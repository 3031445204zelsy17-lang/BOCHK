## 合规检查项
- [ ] 企业是否了解 BOCHK 绿色贷款须符合 GLP（Green Loan Principles）或 SLLP（Sustainability Linked Loan Principles）
- [ ] 可持续发展关联贷款是否需要设定 KPI 和 SPT（Sustainability Performance Targets）

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：了解绿色贷款框架和要求
- 部分满足：知道产品存在
- 不满足：完全不知

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 绿色贷款可用于新项目融资或现有项目再融资
- 可持续发展关联贷款以 KPI 和 SPT 为核心，需与公司核心可持续战略和业务策略紧密对齐
- BOCHK 还提供绿色融资顾问服务，协助企业制定绿色融资框架

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "Green loans are the loans that made available exclusively to finance or re-finance, in whole or in part, new and/or existing eligible Green Projects. Green loans must align with the four core components of the Green Loan Principles."
> —— 来源：BOCHK 官网，Green and Sustainability related Loan 页面（英文原文）

> "Based on Sustainability Linked Loan Principles, sustainability linked loans are the loans that incentivize corporate's achievement of ambitious, predetermined sustainability performance objectives. The corporate's sustainability performance can be assessed through the predefined Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and Sustainability Performance Targets (SPTs)."
> —— 来源：同上
