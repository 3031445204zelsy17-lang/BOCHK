### 2025/2083 对 Annex I 的修订 — 非煅烧高岭土粘土

Reg. (EU) 2025/2083 Art. 1(28) 对 Annex I 中水泥类的 CN 编码具体修订如下：

> "(28) in Annex I, the CN code '2507 00 80 – Other kaolinic clays' is replaced by 'ex 2507 00 80 – Other kaolinic clays except non-calcined kaolinic clays';"
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2025/2083, Art. 1(28)（英文原文，OJ L 2025/2083, 17.10.2025），source_url: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2025/2083/oj/eng

立法理由见 Recital (33)：

> "Goods listed in Annex I to Regulation (EU) 2023/956 include 'other kaolinic clays' in the list of cement goods. While calcined kaolinic clays are carbon-intensive products, this is not the case for non-calcined kaolinic clays. Non-calcined kaolinic clays should therefore be excluded from the scope of Regulation (EU) 2023/956."
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2025/2083, Recital (33)（英文原文）

结论：经 calcined（煅烧）的高岭土粘土仍在 CBAM 范围内；**非煅烧（non-calcined）** 的高岭土粘土已被排除。
