## 合规检查项
- [ ] 企业是否了解 Annex IV 中规定的嵌入排放计算方法的框架
- [ ] 是否区分简单货物（simple goods）与复杂货物（complex goods）的计算方法
- [ ] 是否了解 2025/2083 将部分铝和钢的 finishing processes 排除在系统边界之外

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：了解 Annex IV 框架并能区分 default values 与实际排放数据的适用场景
- 部分满足：了解方法存在但不知具体细节
- 不满足：完全不了解排放计算方法

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 2025/2083 修订了系统边界使其与 EU ETS 对齐（部分铝/钢的 finishing processes 排除）
- 默认值的计算方式也已修改——当无可靠数据时，取排放强度最高的 10 个出口国的均值
- 核查（verification）仅在使用实际排放值时强制

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "ANNEX IV — Methods for calculating embedded emissions for the purpose of Article 7
    1. DEFINITIONS ...
    (a) 'embedded emissions' means direct emissions released during the production of goods, and indirect emissions released during the production of the electricity used during the production process, calculated according to the methods set out in this Annex;
    (b) 'actual emissions' means emissions calculated based on primary data from the production processes of the goods in the installation of origin, including the calculation of emissions from the production of precursors;
    (c) 'default values' means values derived from secondary data, including averages"
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2023/956, Annex IV（英文原文，OJ L 130, 16.5.2023, p. 52）

> "the system boundaries of production processes, which shall be aligned with those covered by the EU ETS"
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2025/2083, Art. 1(6)(c)，替换 Reg. 2023/956 Art. 7(7)(a)（英文原文）
