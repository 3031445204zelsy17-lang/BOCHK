## 合规检查项
- [ ] 发行人是否在其 ESG 报告中按 Part D 要求披露 Scope 1 及 Scope 2 GHG 排放量（以吨 CO2 当量计的绝对总排放量）
- [ ] 发行人是否披露 GHG 排放的测量方法（参照《温室气体核算体系：企业核算与报告标准（2004）》）
- [ ] Scope 2 是否同时披露基于地理位置（location-based）的排放量

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：ESG 报告包含 Scope 1 和 Scope 2 排放量，且按 para 29 要求说明了测量方法和核算标准
- 部分满足：仅披露其中一项，或未说明测量方法 / 核算标准
- 不满足：未披露任何 Scope 1/2 排放数据，或理由不充分

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 中小企业发行人常缺乏 GHG 排放核算经验，不熟悉《温室气体核算体系》的采用要求
- 部分企业未意识到 Scope 1（直接排放）与 Scope 2（能源间接排放）的区分
- 未披露 location-based 与 market-based Scope 2 的区别

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "(1) An issuer must disclose its Scope 1 greenhouse gas emissions and Scope 2 greenhouse gas emissions pursuant to paragraphs 28(a), 28(b) and 29 on a mandatory basis."
> —— 来源：Appendix C2, Part D, para 17(1)（英文原文）

> "An issuer shall disclose its absolute gross greenhouse gas emissions generated during the reporting period, expressed as metric tons of CO2 equivalent, classified as: (a) Scope 1 greenhouse gas emissions; (b) Scope 2 greenhouse gas emissions; and (c) Scope 3 greenhouse gas emissions."
> —— 来源：Appendix C2, Part D, para 28（英文原文）

> "measure its greenhouse gas emissions in accordance with the Greenhouse Gas Protocol: A Corporate Accounting and Reporting Standard (2004) unless required by a jurisdictional authority or another exchange on which the issuer is listed to use a different method for measuring greenhouse gas emissions"
> —— 来源：Appendix C2, Part D, para 29(a)（英文原文）
