## 合规检查项
- [ ] 发行人是否披露排放物相关政策和遵守法律的情况
- [ ] 是否披露各类型排放物及相应排放数据
- [ ] 是否披露有害废弃物总量（吨）及强度
- [ ] 是否披露无害废弃物总量（吨）及强度

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：完整披露 Aspect A1 下全部 KPI（A1.1、A1.3、A1.4、A1.5、A1.6）
- 部分满足：仅披露部分 KPI
- 不满足：未披露

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- Note 明确 Air emissions 包括 NOx, SOx 及国家法规管制的其他污染物，部分企业仅关注 CO2

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "General Disclosure: Information on: (a) the policies; and (b) compliance with relevant laws and regulations that have a significant impact on the issuer relating to air emissions, discharges into water and land, and generation of hazardous and non-hazardous waste.
   Note: Air emissions include NOx, SOx, and other pollutants regulated under national laws and regulations. Hazardous wastes are those defined by national regulations.
   KPI A1.1 The types of emissions and respective emissions data.
   KPI A1.3 Total hazardous waste produced (in tonnes) and, where appropriate, intensity.
   KPI A1.4 Total non-hazardous waste produced (in tonnes) and, where appropriate, intensity.
   KPI A1.5 Description of emission target(s) set and steps taken to achieve them.
   KPI A1.6 Description of how hazardous and non-hazardous wastes are handled, and a description of reduction target(s) set and steps taken to achieve them."
> —— 来源：Appendix C2, Part C, Subject Area A, Aspect A1（英文原文）
