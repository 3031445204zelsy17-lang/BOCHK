## 合规检查项
- [ ] 企业出口至欧盟的货物是否属于 Annex I 清单中的六类之一
- [ ] 是否了解 2025/2083 引入的 de minimis 豁免门槛（50 吨/年累计净重，不适用于电力和氢）

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：能正确判断自身出口产品是否在 CBAM 范围内
- 部分满足：不确定但正在核实
- 不满足：完全不了解适用范围

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- Annex I 按 CN 八位编码分类，企业需核查自己的 HS/CN 编码
- 2025/2083 修订后，原 €150/批次的低值豁免已被 50 吨/年累计净重门槛取代

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "1. This Regulation applies to goods listed in Annex I originating in a third country, where those goods, or processed products from those goods resulting from the inward processing procedure referred to in Article 256 of Regulation (EU) No 952/2013, are imported into the customs territory of the Union."
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2023/956, Art. 2(1)（英文原文）

> "3. By way of derogation from paragraphs 1 and 2, this Regulation shall not apply to goods to be moved or used in the context of military activities pursuant to Article 1, point (49), of Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2015/2446."
> —— 来源：Reg. (EU) 2023/956, Art. 2(3)，经 Reg. (EU) 2025/2083 Art. 1(1)(a) 替换（英文原文，OJ L 2025/2083, 17.10.2025）
