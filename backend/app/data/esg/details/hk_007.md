## 合规检查项
- [ ] 发行人是否披露是否在决策中（如投资决策、转移定价、情景分析）应用碳价格
- [ ] 若应用，是否披露每吨 GHG 排放的碳价格

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：披露了是否应用内部碳定价及具体价格（若适用），或提供了明确的否定声明
- 部分满足：仅提及使用但未说明具体价格
- 不满足：未做任何披露

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 多数中小企业尚未建立内部碳定价机制，但需提供"适当的否定声明"

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "An issuer shall disclose: (a) an explanation of whether and how the issuer is applying a carbon price in decision-making (for example, investment decisions, transfer pricing, and scenario analysis); and (b) the price of each metric tonne of greenhouse gas emissions the issuer uses to assess the costs of its greenhouse gas emissions; or an appropriate negative statement that the issuer does not apply a carbon price in decision-making."
> —— 来源：Appendix C2, Part D, para 34（英文原文）
