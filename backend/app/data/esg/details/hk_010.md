## 合规检查项
- [ ] 发行人是否披露面临气候相关转型风险的资产或业务活动金额及百分比
- [ ] 是否披露面临气候相关物理风险的资产或业务活动金额及百分比
- [ ] 是否披露与气候相关机会一致的资产或业务活动金额及百分比

## 评估标准   <!-- derived: true，需人工复核 -->
- 满足：以金额和百分比形式披露三个类别的暴露情况
- 部分满足：仅披露了部分类别
- 不满足：未做披露

## SME 常见差距   <!-- derived: true -->
- 发行人可能低估物理风险的暴露范围（如供应链地理位置风险）
- 机会一致性（opportunity alignment）的定义和衡量缺乏统一标准

## 法规原文摘要   <!-- 事实层：verbatim，引号标明 -->
> "30. An issuer shall disclose the amount and percentage of assets or business activities vulnerable to climate-related transition risks.
   31. An issuer shall disclose the amount and percentage of assets or business activities vulnerable to climate-related physical risks.
   32. An issuer shall disclose the amount and percentage of assets or business activities aligned with climate-related opportunities.
   Note: In preparing disclosures to meet the requirements in paragraphs 30 to 32, an issuer shall use all reasonable and supportable information that is available to the issuer at the reporting date without undue cost or effort."
> —— 来源：Appendix C2, Part D, para 30-32（英文原文）
